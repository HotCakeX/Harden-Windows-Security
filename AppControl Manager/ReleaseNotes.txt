Introducing Data Analysis

You can now analyze the App Control/Code Integrity event logs or Microsoft Defender for Endpoint (MDE) Advanced Hunting data via the AppControl Manager. Once the data has been retrieved, you will get a textual summary that explains all of the available data and what they mean, additionally, you will also receive multiple recommended actions that you can take for your specific situation.

Interactive charts are generated to help with data visualization. You will have access to the entire blocked and audited/allowed trend in chronological order to quickly identify the peak points and detect anomalies.

There are also multiple Pie and Bar charts to visualize multiple data points and provide insights that helps you understand your data.

This analysis feature is available for both Event Logs (whether from EVTX or local system) and MDE Advanced hunting data (whether live data from cloud or from local CSV files).

The interactive charts can be downloaded in SVG format.

There are multiple color palettes to choose from in order to personalize the charts available in the analysis page.

Please visit the GitHub for more information: https://github.com/HotCakeX/Harden-Windows-Security
