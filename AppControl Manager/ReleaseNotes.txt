AppControl Manager now includes a dedicated Policy Comparison feature designed to make reviewing, auditing, and validating App Control policies faster and more transparent. You can select two policies either from the Sidebar's policies library or select files from your device, compare their contents side by side in a context-aware, responsive and interactive way and review the exact differences across key policy areas such as EKUs, file rules, signers, settings and so on. 

This feature is built for administrators, security engineers, and policy authors who need a professional, reliable way to compare App Control policies with accuracy, clarity, and minimal effort.

Also updated dependencies to the latest version.
